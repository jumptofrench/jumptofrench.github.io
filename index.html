Jump to French Français

A Creative Way to Learn French by English

Written by Iris Melamed
Illustrated by Shazeb Khan

Introduction

Jump to French Français is using English, the already mastered language, as a springboard for acquiring French, the new language.

It is a series of 5 books, English to French, each on a different topic.

600 new words and expressions in French are learnt in an original creative method based on linguistic principles.

Learn French by English in 75 bilingual rhyming poems.

Practice is included for a quick and better French immersion.

French to English glossary is included for more information.

Voice-over is included for right pronunciation.
